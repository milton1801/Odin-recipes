Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: Cook in a large pot of salted water per the recipe below.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl
  4. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until browned and bubbly.
